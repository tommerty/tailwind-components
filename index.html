About the components

I've developed these to be as simple to copy and paste into your own projects. They are basic by design, and are there to give you a "head start" on your own projects. In making them as simple as possible, I've also made some work with and without further addons, such as FlowBite or DaisyUI.
I may plan on adding additional components in the future with those extensions, but for now, I'm happy with what I've got.

They're all available on my GitHub page under the GNU license, so feel free to use them as you wish, as long as it's covered under the license, of course!

If you come across any problems at all, please open an issue on the page, and I'll get back to you as soon as I can.
Feel free to contribute as well, I'm always looking for new ideas and ways to improve my code.
